About Me

Hello! I’m Shreejeevan Chinnadurai, a passionate and self-driven Electronics and Communication Engineering student with strong interests in IoT development, embedded systems, and automation technologies.

I enjoy building real-world solutions that blend hardware and software — from smart control systems to intelligent monitoring platforms.

Throughout my academic journey, I have developed multiple hands-on projects including IoT-based control systems using ESP32 and MQTT, a smart car parking system with Arduino, a digital library management system using barcode technology, and an antenna design for RF energy harvesting. With a solid foundation in C, C++, Python, PCB Design, and IoT protocols, I am passionate about creating efficient, scalable, and smart solutions. I am always eager to learn new technologies, solve real-world problems, and contribute meaningfully to innovative projects.

My goal is to work on projects that push the boundaries of technology, especially in the areas of IoT, embedded systems, and smart automation.
